Wexford Family Fun Itinerary

Your guide to an unforgettable family adventure in County Wexford!

Day 1: Arrival & Summer Beach Bliss

Upton Court Hotel, Gorey, Co. Wexford

Check into the charming Upton Court Hotel, your cozy base for the week. Settle in and prepare for your Wexford adventure!

Kate's Coffee Shop & Deli, Kilmuckridge, Co. Wexford

Enjoy a delightful lunch at Kate's, known for its fresh sandwiches, pastries, and excellent coffee.

Morriscastle Beach, Kilmuckridge, Co. Wexford

Spend the afternoon building sandcastles, splashing in the waves, and enjoying the golden sands of Morriscastle Beach.

Lawler's of Morriscastle, Kilmuckridge, Co. Wexford

Savour a delicious dinner at Lawler's, a popular local spot with a warm atmosphere and great food.

Day 2: Nature Walk & Wildlife Wonders

Kilmuckridge-Morriscastle Trail, Kilmuckridge, Co. Wexford

Enjoy a refreshing morning walk along this scenic trail, perfect for taking in the fresh air and beautiful coastal views.

Secret Valley Wildlife Park, Clonroche, Enniscorthy, Co. Wexford

Explore a variety of animals and enjoy interactive experiences at this fantastic wildlife park.

Secret Valley Wildlife Park (Reptile Zone/Pony Rides)

Get up close with fascinating reptiles and let the kids enjoy a fun pony ride.

Partridges, Main Street, Gorey, Co. Wexford (Ice Cream)

Cool down with a delicious ice cream treat at Partridges after a day of adventure.

Day 3: Wells House Adventure Day

Wells House & Gardens, Ballyedmond, Gorey, Co. Wexford

Spend a full day exploring the historic house, extensive gardens, and woodlands of Wells House.

Brambles Café, Wells House & Gardens

Enjoy a lovely lunch or snack at Brambles Café, located within the beautiful Wells House grounds.

Glenavon Japanese Garden, Wells House & Gardens

Discover tranquility in the serene Glenavon Japanese Garden, a peaceful oasis within Wells House.

Day 4: Castle Explorers & Crafts

Enniscorthy Castle, Enniscorthy, Co. Wexford

Step back in time at Enniscorthy Castle, a historic landmark with fascinating exhibits and panoramic views.

Tinnaberna Sandhills, Kilmuckridge, Co. Wexford

Explore the unique natural landscape of Tinnaberna Sandhills, perfect for a breezy walk and dune exploration.

Gorey (Pottery Painting or Craft Workshop)

Unleash your creativity with a fun pottery painting or craft workshop in Gorey, perfect for all ages.

Day 5: Festivities & Free Play

Gorey (Summer Market Stalls)

Wander through the vibrant summer market stalls in Gorey, browsing local crafts and delicious treats.

The Greenhouse Café, Kilmuckridge, Co. Wexford (Kids Crafts)

Enjoy a relaxing break at The Greenhouse Café while the kids engage in fun craft activities.

Ballinoulart Beach, Kilmuckridge, Co. Wexford

Discover the serene beauty of Ballinoulart Beach, a perfect spot for a quiet stroll and enjoying nature.

Day 6: Farewell Fun

Kilmuckridge Drama Festival

Experience the local culture with an evening at the Kilmuckridge Drama Festival, showcasing local talent.

Da Paolo Italian Restaurant, Main Street, Gorey, Co. Wexford

Enjoy a final family meal with authentic Italian cuisine at Da Paolo in Gorey.

Wexford Town, Co. Wexford

Before heading home, take some time to explore the historic and charming streets of Wexford Town.
