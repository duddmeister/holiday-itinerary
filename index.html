Wexford Family Fun Itinerary

Your guide to an unforgettable family adventure in County Wexford!

Day 1: Arrival & Summer Beach Bliss

Upton Court Hotel, Gorey, Co. Wexford

Check into the charming Upton Court Hotel, your cozy base for the week. Settle in and prepare for your Wexford adventure! The hotel offers comfortable rooms, friendly service, and is conveniently located near many local attractions. Enjoy a relaxing evening and get ready for a week of exploration and fun with the whole family.

Kate's Coffee Shop & Deli, Kilmuckridge, Co. Wexford

Enjoy a delightful lunch at Kate's, known for its fresh sandwiches, pastries, and excellent coffee. It's a perfect spot for a quick and tasty bite before heading to the beach, offering a relaxed atmosphere for families.

Morriscastle Beach, Kilmuckridge, Co. Wexford

Spend the afternoon building sandcastles, splashing in the waves, and enjoying the golden sands of Morriscastle Beach. Known as "The Golden Mile," it's one of Ireland's longest beaches, perfect for family fun and relaxation.

Lawler's of Morriscastle, Kilmuckridge, Co. Wexford

Savour a delicious dinner at Lawler's, a popular local spot with a warm atmosphere and great food. It's the perfect place to unwind after a day at the beach, offering a menu that caters to all tastes, including kid-friendly options.

Day 2: Nature Walk & Wildlife Wonders

Kilmuckridge-Morriscastle Trail, Kilmuckridge, Co. Wexford

Enjoy a refreshing morning walk along this scenic trail, perfect for taking in the fresh air and beautiful coastal views. The trail offers a gentle path suitable for families, with opportunities to spot local wildlife and enjoy the serene Irish countryside.

Secret Valley Wildlife Park, Clonroche, Enniscorthy, Co. Wexford

Explore a variety of animals and enjoy interactive experiences at this fantastic wildlife park. From exotic species to farm animals, there's something for everyone. Don't miss the daily feeding times and keeper talks for an even more engaging visit!

Secret Valley Wildlife Park (Reptile Zone/Pony Rides)

Get up close with fascinating reptiles in the dedicated zone and let the kids enjoy a fun pony ride. These hands-on experiences are highlights for many visitors, providing unique memories and photo opportunities.

Partridges, Main Street, Gorey, Co. Wexford (Ice Cream)

Cool down with a delicious ice cream treat at Partridges after a day of adventure. This local favourite offers a wide selection of flavours, perfect for a sweet ending to your wildlife park visit.

Day 3: Wells House Adventure Day

Wells House & Gardens, Ballyedmond, Gorey, Co. Wexford

Spend a full day exploring the historic house, extensive gardens, and woodlands of Wells House. This grand estate offers beautiful walks, a children's playground, and various attractions, ensuring a day full of discovery for the entire family.

Brambles Café, Wells House & Gardens

Enjoy a lovely lunch or snack at Brambles Café, located within the beautiful Wells House grounds. It's a convenient and pleasant spot to refuel during your exploration of the estate.

Glenavon Japanese Garden, Wells House & Gardens

Discover tranquility in the serene Glenavon Japanese Garden, a peaceful oasis within Wells House. This beautifully designed garden offers a calming escape, perfect for quiet reflection amidst your busy family day.

Day 4: Castle Explorers & Crafts

Enniscorthy Castle, Enniscorthy, Co. Wexford

Step back in time at Enniscorthy Castle, a historic landmark with fascinating exhibits and panoramic views. Explore its dungeons, climb to the rooftop, and learn about the rich history of the area, including its role in the 1798 Rebellion.

Tinnaberna Sandhills, Kilmuckridge, Co. Wexford

Explore the unique natural landscape of Tinnaberna Sandhills, perfect for a breezy walk and dune exploration. This beautiful coastal area is a hidden gem, offering stunning views and a chance to connect with nature.

Gorey (Pottery Painting or Craft Workshop)

Unleash your creativity with a fun pottery painting or craft workshop in Gorey, perfect for all ages. It's a wonderful indoor activity, especially on a rainy day, allowing everyone to create a unique souvenir.

Day 5: Festivities & Free Play

Gorey (Summer Market Stalls)

Wander through the vibrant summer market stalls in Gorey, browsing local crafts and delicious treats. Depending on the time of year, you might find various local markets offering unique goods, entertainment, and a lively atmosphere.

The Greenhouse Café, Kilmuckridge, Co. Wexford (Kids Crafts)

Enjoy a relaxing break at The Greenhouse Café while the kids engage in fun craft activities. This charming café often hosts creative sessions, making it a great spot for both parents and children to relax and play.

Ballinoulart Beach, Kilmuckridge, Co. Wexford

Discover the serene beauty of Ballinoulart Beach, a perfect spot for a quiet stroll and enjoying nature. This less-crowded beach offers a peaceful escape, ideal for collecting seashells or simply enjoying the coastal breeze.

Day 6: Farewell Fun

Kilmuckridge Drama Festival

Experience the local culture with an evening at the Kilmuckridge Drama Festival, showcasing local talent. Check local listings for performance schedules during your visit for an entertaining night out.

Da Paolo Italian Restaurant, Main Street, Gorey, Co. Wexford

Enjoy a final family meal with authentic Italian cuisine at Da Paolo in Gorey. This popular restaurant offers a delightful menu with classic dishes, perfect for a memorable last dinner in Wexford.

Wexford Town, Co. Wexford

Before heading home, take some time to explore the historic and charming streets of Wexford Town. Visit the iconic Selskar Abbey, stroll along the quays, or explore the local shops for souvenirs of your trip.
